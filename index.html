Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Answers for Real People

We know you have questions. Here are straightforward answers to what most people ask about OpsGate.

How much does the 14 day pilot cost?
Absolutely nothing. The 14 day pilot is completely free, with no credit card required and no hidden charges. We want you to experience OpsGate's value firsthand before making any decisions. After the pilot, if you love it, we have simple pricing based on how many sites you want to protect.
Is OpsGate a cloud service or do I install it myself?
Right now, OpsGate is designed to run on your own server or VPS using Docker (think of it like a self contained app). We are starting with this approach to make sure it works perfectly for WordPress agencies. A cloud version where we handle everything for you is coming later once we perfect the system.
Will OpsGate mess with my current WordPress hosting?
No changes to your hosting at all. OpsGate works alongside what you already have. It talks to your WordPress, your FTP/cPanel, and your SSL setup, but it does not force you to move hosts or change platforms.
Do I need to be a technical expert to use OpsGate?
Not at all. While OpsGate is powerful, we have made it straightforward to set up. Our pilot includes step by step guidance, and once running, it works automatically. If you can install a Docker container (we provide clear instructions), you can run OpsGate.
What happens after the 14 day free pilot ends?
You get to keep all the evidence and reports we generated during the pilot. If you decide OpsGate is right for you, we have simple per site or bundle pricing. If not, you simply stop using it, no questions asked. There is no automatic billing or tricky cancellation process.
Does OpsGate replace my current security plugins?
No, OpsGate works with your existing tools. Think of it as the coordinator and proof generator. You keep using your favorite security plugins; OpsGate ties everything together and gives you one clear story of what actually happened across all your sites.
